Coming to you from (just) within the beltway!

The Perl Conference 2017 in Washington, DC (teeecchhnniiccallyyy Alexandria, VA) is a high-quality, inexpensive technical conference with its roots in the Perl Mongers user groups. The conference celebrates the Perl programming language and is meant to be accessible to anyone, regardless of experience, yet valuable to even the most skilled of programmers.

Each year the conference attracts hundreds of programmers from around the world, including luminaries such as Larry Wall, Randal Schwartz, and Damian Conway.
